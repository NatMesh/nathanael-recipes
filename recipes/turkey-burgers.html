Turkey Burgers

Whether you've been searching for the best turkey burger, need to use up the ground turkey lurking in your freezer, or are simply tired of beef burgers, this is the recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey
  • diced onion
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Eggs
  • minced garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Take all the ingredients and mix them well in a bowl.
  2. Once mixed form the turkey into burger patty shapes and cook for around 10min and internal temperature is at least 165 degress F.
  3. Once cooked add your sauces of choice, veggies, bun and these are ready to eat!
