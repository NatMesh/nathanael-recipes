Banana Pancakes

Crowd-pleasing banana pancakes made from scratch that are ready in minutes. A fun twist on ordinary pancakes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • An Egg
  • Milk
  • Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Bananas

Steps

  1. Mix all your dry and wet ingredients in seperate bowls, then once done combine contents in dry bowl into the wet bowl.
  2. Once mixed pour batter onto a lightly oiled pan on medium-high heat and cook for a few minutes on each side.
  3. Once both sides have a golden brown color they are ready to serve.
